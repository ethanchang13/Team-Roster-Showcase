Our Legacy

The story of the Golden State Warriors' rise to greatness

Our Journey

Since 1946, the Golden State Warriors have been a cornerstone of NBA history, evolving from Philadelphia to a global phenomenon in San Francisco.

With a revolutionary approach to basketball, led by coach Steve Kerr, the Warriors have pioneered the three-point era, securing 8 NBA titles, including 2022.

Achievements

  • NBA Champions (2015, 2017, 2018, 2022)
  • Western Conference Leaders (2015-2019, 2022, 2025)
  • Record 73-9 Season (2016)

2025 Stats

  • Record: 45-15
  • Points Per Game: 120.2
  • Three-Point %: 40.1%
