Our Legacy

The story of the Golden State Warriors' rise to greatness

Our History

The Golden State Warriors, established in 1946, have a rich history filled with triumphs and challenges. From their early days in Philadelphia to their current status as a powerhouse in the NBA, the Warriors have consistently pushed the boundaries of excellence.

With a commitment to teamwork, innovation, and community, the Warriors have become a symbol of resilience and success in professional sports.

Our Journey

Since 1946, the Golden State Warriors have been a cornerstone of NBA history, evolving from Philadelphia to a global phenomenon in San Francisco.

With a revolutionary approach to basketball, led by coach Steve Kerr, the Warriors have pioneered the three-point era, securing 7 NBA titles.

Achievements

  • NBA Champions
    (1947, 1956, 1975, 2015, 2017, 2018, 2022)
  • Western Conference Leaders
    (1975, 2015-2019, 2022)
  • Record 73-9 Season (2016)

2025 Stats

  • Record: 48-34
  • Points Per Game: 113.8
  • Three-Point %: 36.6%

Our Community

The Warriors are not just a team; they are a community. Through various initiatives, they give back to the Bay Area, supporting education, health, and youth programs.

Their commitment to social responsibility is as strong as their dedication to winning on the court.

Our Future

The Warriors are poised for continued success with a blend of seasoned veterans and emerging stars. Their focus on innovation and teamwork ensures they remain at the forefront of the NBA.

As they look to the future, the Warriors remain committed to their legacy of excellence and community impact.
